Return Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Product received is damaged or defective
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Product is expired or near expiry
  • Return request made within 7 days of delivery

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used products
  • Products without original packaging
  • Products returned after 7 days
  • Products damaged due to misuse

3. Return Process

  1. Contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide order details and reason for return
  3. Share photos of the product (if damaged)
  4. Our team will verify and approve the return
  5. Schedule a pickup or drop at our location

4. Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after receiving the returned product
  • Refund will be credited to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable (except for wrong/damaged products)

5. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Damaged products
  • Subject to product availability
