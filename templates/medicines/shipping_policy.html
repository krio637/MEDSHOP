Shipping Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Shipping Coverage

We deliver across India. Currently, we do not offer international shipping.

2. Delivery Time

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days

Delivery times may vary during peak seasons, festivals, or unforeseen circumstances.

3. Shipping Charges

  • Free Shipping: On orders above ₹500
  • Standard Shipping: ₹50 for orders below ₹500
  • Express Delivery: Additional charges may apply

4. Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive a tracking number via email/SMS once your order is shipped.

5. Tracking Your Order

You can track your order using:

  • The tracking link sent to your email
  • Your account dashboard under "Orders"
  • Contacting our customer support

6. Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partners will make up to 3 delivery attempts. If delivery fails after 3 attempts, the order will be returned to us.
